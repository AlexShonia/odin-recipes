Yalia

Description

Cool recipe for Yalia a delicious dish perfect for cold winter days. High protein and very low ingredient recipe. It's healthy and perfect for athletes. Est time ~ 3hours. Recipe is very easy to follow (even my 1.5yo kid can do it) perfect for dummy dum dums

  • 1 kg pork
  • 1 kg beef
  • 1 kg onions
  • Single pomegranate
  • Salt,pepper and other spices

Steps

  1. Add all of pork,beef and onions into large pot
  2. Turn the heat to lowest and let it cook for 3 hours while lid is on
  3. When the meat is nice and soft add pomegranate bits
  4. Add salt, red pepper, yellow flower, coriander powder
  5. Let it cook for 5 more minutes with lid off
  6. Enjoy
